About Our Market

Bringing Freshness to Your Table Since 2010

Fresh Harvest Farmers Market is a community-focused marketplace that connects local farmers, artisans, and food producers with health-conscious consumers. We believe in sustainable agriculture, supporting local economies, and providing access to fresh, nutritious food.

Every Saturday from 8am to 2pm, rain or shine, we gather at the town square to celebrate the bounty of our region. Our vendors are carefully selected to ensure the highest quality products and ethical practices.

Join us for a vibrant community experience where you can meet the people who grow your food, learn about sustainable practices, and enjoy the freshest seasonal offerings.
